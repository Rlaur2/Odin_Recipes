Beans

Description

Black beans are easy to make with an instant pressure cooker. They freeze really well for future meals and pair very nicely with chicken and white rice for an authentic Dominican meal.

Ingredients

  • Black beans
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Chicken boullion cube
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Soak beans in water overnight.
  2. Cook beans on high pressure for 30 mins.
  3. When the countdown timer reaches 10 minutes, heat a pan with vegetable oil and sautee these ingredients for the remaining 10 minutes.
    • Onions
    • Bell peppers
    • Garlic
    • Cilantro
    • Tomato paste
  4. After the beans are done, take 25% of the beans and puree them in a blender. Add the pureed beans back into the main pot.
  5. Add the satuee'd vegetables to the main pot and cook until the beans are reduced, about 30 mins. (Use the sautee function on the instant pot)
  6. Season with chicken boillion, adobo seasonin, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Enjoy!
