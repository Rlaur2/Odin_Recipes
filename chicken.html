Chicken

Description

This is a great recipe for Dominican-style chicken. Great for family or gatherings. Include white rice and beans for an authentic Dominican meal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks and/or thighs
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Cilantro
  • Soy sauce
  • Oregano
  • Chicken boullion cube
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Adobo seasoning

Steps

  1. Wash chicken with cool tap water before preparing
  2. Marinate chicken overnight with the seasonings and soy sauce
  3. Add chopped onions and peppers to chicken
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a pan on high
  5. Add chicken to pan then move the chicken to the edges of the pan
  6. Add water to the container that had the chicken and mix
  7. Spoon chicken container water onto chicken every 5 minutes and turn chicken
  8. After 15 minutes add the contents of the chicken bowl to the pan and cover pan
  9. Lower heat to Low-Med and cook for 30 minutes
  10. Add freshly chopped cilantro when chicken is done
  11. Enjoy!
