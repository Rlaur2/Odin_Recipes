White rice

Description

Making perfectly cooked white rice is simple when using a rice cooker. Here are the simple ingredients and steps on how to make some. White rice pairs extremely well with chicken and beans!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Add desired rice with the included measuring cup.
  2. Add water up to suggested line depending on how many cups of rice was added.
  3. Salt to taste (optional)
  4. Cover rice cooker and turn on.
  5. The rice can cook unattended and will turn off when ready.
  6. Enjoy perfectly cooked rice!
